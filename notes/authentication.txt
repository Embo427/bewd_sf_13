authentication
  - who you are
  - validating w/ username/password
  - tracking identity in a session
      - fingerprint on what you're doing through your browser
  - keeping between visits w/ cookie
  - protects the site w/ security encryption

storing passwords
  - passwords should never be stored in "plain text"
  - always use "hashing encryption" on passwords before storing them in database
    - done automatically with auth gems

authorization - what you have access to
